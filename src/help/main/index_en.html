Note: This version of efa is a development version, which comes with lots of new features but has not yet been tested thoroughly. It is intended for users who want to try out the new features as early as possible and would like to support further development by supplying feedback. User who are mostly concerned about a stable version of efa should use the version 1.8.3 for productive environments instead.

Beta Test of efa2
The official Version 2.0 of efa in planned to be released in early 2012. Until the final release there will be several pre-released development versions.
Your Feedback as a user of those early version is therefore very important to help develop and further improve efa 2.0. Please post your ideas and bug reports in the discussion groups at kenai.com/projects/efa or actively join the development! Thank you!
